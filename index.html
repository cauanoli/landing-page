Study chinese for free online

Want to learn a new language but short on funds for a course? This guide is for self-study, focusing on standard Chinese.
Tips here may also provide help learning any other language. Enjoy the journey!

Resources

Refold method

The Refold method is a great free guide on language learning while having fun. It is an essential guide if you want to start learning any language. Give it a try!

Heavenly Path

A guide made by a community of learners. It contain great guides with many media suggetions. It is great throught your journey from begginer to mastery.

Beijing University Courses

A series of courses following the HSK structure. The professors are great and the course is very well structured. You you take it for free without certificate.

Anki decks

If you want to learn chinese having a tool such as Anki is essential. It helps you learn and remember new characters with spaced repetition. Best of all, Anki is free and open-source!
